The Sierra Challenge Express is the Central Valley's premier running and walking non-profit club geared toward for runners and walker of all abilities and ages. We serve the community by promoting a healthy lifestyle, fostering griendship through social gatherings, and all of our proceeds go back to the community.

Individual and family memberships are available at a low yearly cost, and minors under 18 are free
