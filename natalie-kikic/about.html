ABOUT

Originally from California, Natalie Kikić grew up on stories of her parents' life in the former Yugoslavia. Her passion for her heritage led her to earn a Bachelor's in Slavic Languages & Literatures from the University of California, Berkeley. Natalie's work explores Balkan folklore, belonging, and hauntings, both ghostly and romantic.

When not writing, Natalie can typically be found wrapped in a blanket with a cup of chai, meticulously planning her next travel itinerary, or hyperfixating on a new hobby. She currently resides in Massachusetts with her husband and two polydactyl cats.

(Kikić is pronounced Kee-kitch)
