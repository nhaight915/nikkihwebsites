HOUSE OF LAVENDER

Adult, Gothic Horror-Romance

Beware the House of Lavender —
something dark blooms there.

2025,Croatia:

Petra Babić has always longed to know more about her mother's past before coming to America. All she's inherited are tales of loss, the untimely demise of her father and grandparents. But after her mother's death, Petra discovers a shocking secret: her grandmother is actually alive. Searching for answers, Petra ventures to her ancestral home, known for its sprawling lavender field — and the spirit that haunts it.

1895:

Slut. Sinner. Witch. No matter how many people Magdalena Derokov heals, the rumors persist. So when she's asked to heal the matriarch of the grand House of Lavender, she sees a chance to gain a new reputation, and maybe even love. Yet the malaise afflicting the house is more than mere sickness. When her healing efforts fail, resulting in heartbreak and betrayal, she seeks eternal revenge against the family that ruined her.

Back in the present, Petra investigates the hauntings with the help of a handsome local named Luka. But when she uncovers Magdalena's mission to kill all those her family loves, she realizes Luka may be her next victim. To finally end her family's curse, Petra must vanquish Magdalena — and confront the true darkness of her inheritance.

"This House is her inheritance.

Her roots.

Her blood.

She is nothing without it."
