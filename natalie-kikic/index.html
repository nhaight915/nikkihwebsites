For the Price of One Nightmare is a dark fantasy short story that explores the concept of a mora in Croatian folklore - a sinister female spirit that brings sleep paralysis and nightmares. Read it in Issue Eleven of Haven Spec magazine.

About Natalie Kikić

Natalie Kikić is a speculative fiction writer and daughter of immigrants from the former Yugoslavia. Originally from California, she has called many places home, and currently resides in Nova Scotia, Canada with her husband and two polydactyl cats. Holding a Bachelor’s in Slavic Languages & Literatures, Natalie primarily writes haunting fiction inspired by her heritage.

Current WIP: House of Lavendar

When Petra Babić travels to Croatia to meet her grandmother for the first time, she expects to uncover the metaphorical ghosts of her mother’s past. But the ghost she finds in her ancestral home is real—and she wants revenge.

HOUSE OF LAVENDER is a dual POV Adult gothic horror with romantic elements. It combines the eerie atmosphere of Silvia Moreno-Garcia’s Mexican Gothic with the vengeful spirit of Isabel Cañas’s The Hacienda, but with a Croatian folklore twist.

Natalie is currently revising this novel and will soon be seeking representation.
